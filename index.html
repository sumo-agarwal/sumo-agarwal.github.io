About me

PROFESSIONAL PATH

A final year undergraduate student at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, majoring in Electrical Engineering. Well I'm just a guy who takes pleasure in making a silly little application or designing a lane navigation algorithm for driverless car; because what I love about it is the process of engineering, simple yet creative solutions, from ground up to problems that provoke me, however small or big they be. Over the past years I have developed interests in the fields of Computational Imaging and Optics such as Light Field Technology, Medical Imaging, Imaging through scattering media and 3 dimensional image stiching. The prospects and capabilites of these fields in solving problems that are pertinent to daily lives is energizing. Check out my github and facebook to know more! Apart from spending my time trying to improve human-computer interaction, I am involved with Kgpian Game Theory Society as its head member. Click here and here to know more about the society. Also, I am fond of travelling and trekking and wouldn't miss for the world to get out and explore the world.
