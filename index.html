PREVIOUS JOBS

Illumination-invariant Face recognition by fusing thermal and visual images via gradient transfer

Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur
AUGUST 2018 - OCTOBER 2018

Proposed a notion that fusion of visual and thermal images gives better results for face recognition under low illuminant conditions. The fused image is processed in a way as to keep the main intensity distribution in the infrared image and the gradient variation in the visible image. A new method of face detection called the biggest blob extraction method is applied on thermal images. A new dataset of thermal and visual images under the same alignment and ground truth perception for 200 subjects is released. As an extension to the given work, the relation between the infrared modality of the forehead region and the stress level of the subjects subjected to time changes is formulated and presented through an android app.

Grading Tumor Malignancy via Deep Bidirectional LSTM on Graph Manifold Encoded Histopathological Images(HI)

Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur
MAY 2018 - AUGUST 2018

An automated Breast cancer categorization framework employing Bidirectional LSTM (Bi-LSTM) on manifold encoded HI of the quasi-isometric topological space is proposed. Manifold learning via Landmark ISOMAP (L-ISOMAP) on stain normalized unfolded HIs have been used to model its latent multidimensional structural dynamics.
